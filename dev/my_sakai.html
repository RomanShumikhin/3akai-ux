Accept myBerkeley Beta Program

Welcome to the myBerkeley Beta.

myBerkeley is a program designed to bring many campus online services under one roof. The current beta has a limited number of features. It will allow Berkeley to send you reminders of important tasks and events, as well as more general messages.

What you should know before joining this program:

  • You may receive multiple messages for the same task or event.
  • Messages may be deleted at any time due to system or project events.
  • The design and features presented may change at any time.
  • By participating in this program you may be invited to participate in surveys and focus groups to help us determine the effectiveness of the design and features presented here.
